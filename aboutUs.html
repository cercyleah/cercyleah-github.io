Introduction

Our history?

The Martin De Porres Catholic Church in Atonsu-SLine was established in 1980 by 4 Catholic priest. It was commissioned by the then govenor. Today it forms part of the Amakom Deanery of the Kumasi Archdiocese.

Our leaders?

It is headed by Rev. Fr. Jude Anin Agyei, the Parish Priest, who is assisted by Rev. Fr. Stephen Baidoo Junior, the Assistant Parish Priest. The Parish is blessed with a number of priest namely Rev. Fr. Michael Obuo Mensah Fobi, Rev. Fr. Emmanuel Kwadwo Boakye, Rev. Fr. Mark Opoku Acheampong, Rev. Fr. James Alaya, Rev. Fr. Anthony Otu Abban, Rev. Fr. Kirk Okyere Siabour, Rev. Fr. Desmond Duruye and Rev. Fr. Emmanuel Mensah Boateng.

Our schedule?

The church has about 400 members and still counting. We have 2 masses on Sunday at 6:30am(English Service) and 9:30am(Twi Service). We also have morning mass every morning at 6:00am from Monday to Saturday. Cofession is done every Saturday after 1pm. Book an appointment for confession.

Mission

Catholic Church's mission World Wide?

Catholic Answers is a media ministry that serves Christ by explaining and defending the Catholic faith. We help Catholics grow in their faith; we bring former Catholics home; and we lead non-Catholics into the fullness of the truth.

Learn more

Catholic Church's mission Kumasi Archdiocese?

  • To proclaim the Good News is our mission; by building on the foundation of the Catholic Archdiocese of Kumasi through diligent formation of Christ's Faithful.
  • To consciously learn from Scriptures and Tradition.
  • To courageously address issues of today and proactively anticipate our spiritual, human and material developments with biblical values as guide.

Learn more

Our mission?

To continually shape the lives of christian to follow the example of Jesus Christ by being kind and generous to all those around us

Vision

Catholic Church Vision World Wide?

Our goal is to be the most trusted and accessible source for sharing, explaining, and defending the Catholic faith through as many media as possible: from personal contact to the most innovative means of mass communication.

Learn more

Catholic Church Vision Kumasi Archidiocese?

The Catholic Archdiocese of Kumasi envisions a Eucharistic Community for holistic selfless service to God and humanity in Evangelization.

Learn more

Our Vision?

We envision Christians who are generous givers and are committed to showing acts of love and kindness to their neighbours.
